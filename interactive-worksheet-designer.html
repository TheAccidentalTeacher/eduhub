Important Safety Rules

  1. Know your personal information - Your full name, address, and a phone number to call in case of emergency.
  2. Stay with trusted adults - Always tell an adult where you are going.
  3. Use the buddy system - Play and walk with friends, not alone.
  4. Know how to call for help - Learn how to call 911 in an emergency.
  5. Practice fire safety - Stop, drop, and roll if your clothes catch fire.
  6. Be careful around strangers - Never go with someone you don't know.
